I'm a PhD-trained research coordinator with over eight years of experience designing and executing multi-site research projects across academic and industry settings. My work sits at the intersection of cognitive science, data strategy, and human-centred research.

From collaborating with teams at NYU, the University of Melbourne, Meta, and Qualcomm, to leading A/B testing initiatives at Loblaw Digital, I bring rigorous methodology and practical execution to every project.

I specialize in translating complex data into actionable insights — through statistical modelling, training program design, and stakeholder reporting.
